01 / About

Engineering grounded in how businesses actually work.

For more than four years, I have been building and maintaining internal software at a shipping company in Karachi, including a full HRMS used across day-to-day operations.

My work covers employee records, attendance, leave, payroll with automated tax calculations, approvals, reporting, and operational workflows. I work across the full lifecycle: process mapping, requirements, architecture, implementation, testing, and improvement.

Before software development, I spent five years as an IT Officer at Askari Bank supporting enterprise systems across branches. That background shaped how I think about reliability, users, stakeholders, and the realities around production software.
